A Multi-Chain Defi Community Token.

KogeCoin is a deflationary community token that launched via airdrop to all early QuickSwap users on Polygon who had around 4 weeks to claim.

The original KogeCoin team built KogeFarm.io, an auto-compounding utility built for DeFi users. The KogeCoin project has since been governed by a DAO, with other projects being proposed by community members.
