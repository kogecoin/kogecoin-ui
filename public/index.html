Deflationary. Community-Launched. Built on Matic/Polygon.

KogeCoin is a deflationary community coin for early Quickswap adopters. All Quickswap users from before timestamp are eligible for a Genesis airdrop that is equal to 22.5% of the total initial supply. To reward HODLERs and increase adoption, another 75% of tokens will be airdropped to token holders 1 month after Genesis. For every 1 KogeCoin you hold at that time, you’ll get another at least another 3!

Transactions will incur a 2% fee, about half of which will be burned and another half distributed to all holders.
