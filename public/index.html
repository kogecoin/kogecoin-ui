Deflationary. Community-Launched. QuickSwap Meme Coin.

KogeCoin is a deflationary token for the nascent QuickSwap/Matic Community. Anyone who made a transaction on Quickswap before May 3, 2021 5PM UTC is eligible for a Genesis airdrop that is equal to 96% of the total initial supply. The initial airdrop will end around June 3, 2021 (block number 1622763836).

Too often, “fair launched” tokens get sniped by bots who then dump on the rest of us. By airdropping to everyone in the community, KogeCoin avoids this problem and allows the average user to benefit from a launch.

The devs are fully committed to KogeCoin and the Polygon/Matic defi space. Many airdropped tokens end up having lasting value due to their attachment to the community (e.g. AuroraCoin, $8M market cap). We view KogeCoin as a token that will allow us to grow with the community. Holders of KogeCoin will benefit from many projects in the farming/defi space to come!

Similar to other deflationary tokens, transactions will carry a 2% fee, about half of which will be burned and another half distributed to all holders. It is always better to claim early to start collecting the transaction fees.

Another benefit of holding is that all tokens that were allocated to the initial airdrop but went unclaimed for 1 month following Genesis, along with any transaction fees they accumulated, will be redistributed to existing token holders in a second airdrop.
