Deflationary. Community-Launched. QuickSwap Meme Coin.

KogeCoin is a deflationary community coin for early Quickswap adopters. All Quickswap users from before timestamp are eligible for a Genesis airdrop that is equal to 23% of the total initial supply. To reward HODLERs, 75% of the total initial supply, along with any transaction fees it accumulates, will be airdropped to token holders 1 month after Genesis. This means that every 1 KOGECOIN you hold, you’ll get another at least another 3 KOGECOIN. Fiat exchange listings will be pursued after the HODLERs' airdrop.

Transactions will incur a 2% fee, about half of which will be burned and another half distributed to all holders. Transaction fees accrued to the Genesis airdrop wallet will be burned. It is always be better to claim early to start collecting the transaction fees!
