Deflationary. Community-Launched. QuickSwap Meme Coin.

KogeCoin is a deflationary community token for early Quickswap adopters. Everyone who has made a transaction on Quickswap before May 3, 2021 5PM UTC is eligible for a Genesis airdrop that is equal to 23% of the total initial supply. You can check if you’re on the airdrop list here.

Another 75% of the total initial supply, along with any transaction fees it accumulates, will be airdropped to token holders 1 month after Genesis. This means that every 1 KOGECOIN you HODL, you’ll get another at least another 3 KOGECOIN. Fiat exchange listings will be pursued after this second airdrop.

Transactions will incur a 2% fee, about half of which will be burned and another half distributed to all holders. Transaction fees accrued to the Genesis airdrop wallet will be burned. It is always be better to claim early to start collecting the transaction fees!
